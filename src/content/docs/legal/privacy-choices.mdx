# NanoKnow Privacy Choices

**Last updated:** May 3, 2026

We respect your right to control how your personal information is collected, used, and shared. This page explains the choices available to you and how to exercise them.

## 1. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

NanoKnow does **not** sell your personal information. We may use third-party analytics services that collect usage data to help us improve our site. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), certain data sharing with these third parties may be considered "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes.

**To opt out:** Contact us at [privacy@nanoknow.org](mailto:privacy@nanoknow.org) with the subject line "Do Not Sell or Share" and we will process your request within 15 business days.

## 2. Categories of Personal Information We Collect

| Category | Examples | Source | Purpose |
|----------|----------|--------|---------|
| Identifiers | IP address, browser type | Collected automatically | Site analytics and security |
| Internet activity | Pages visited, session duration, referral URL | Collected automatically | Site improvement |
| Geolocation | General location (country/region) | Derived from IP address | Content optimization |

## 3. Third-Party Services

We may share anonymized, aggregated usage data with the following categories of third parties:

- **Analytics providers** — to understand site usage patterns
- **Hosting infrastructure** — to deliver and secure the site

We do **not** share personal information with data brokers or advertisers.

## 4. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

### Right to Know
You may request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources of that information, the business purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom it is shared.

### Right to Delete
You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain legal exceptions.

### Right to Correct
You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

### Right to Opt Out of Sharing
You may direct us to stop sharing your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

### Right to Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights. You will not receive different pricing, quality of service, or access based on your choices.

## 5. How to Submit a Request

You may exercise any of the rights above by:

- **Email:** [admin@nanoknow.org](mailto:admin@nanoknow.org)
- **Subject line:** Include the specific right you are exercising (e.g., "Right to Delete," "Right to Know")

### Verification
To protect your privacy, we will verify your identity before fulfilling any request. We may ask you to confirm information we already hold or provide additional identifying details.

### Authorized Agents
You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf. The agent must provide written proof of authorization, and we may still verify your identity directly.

### Response Timeline
We will acknowledge your request within 10 business days and provide a substantive response within 45 calendar days. If additional time is needed, we will notify you of an extension of up to 45 additional days.

## 6. Opt-Out Preferences

### Analytics & Usage Data
We use analytics to understand how our site is used. You may opt out of analytics tracking by:

- Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings
- Using a browser extension that blocks analytics scripts
- Contacting us at the email above

### Communications
If you have subscribed to communications from us, you may opt out at any time by using the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly.

## 7. Changes to This Page

We may update this page to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
