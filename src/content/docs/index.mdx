<Section title='<span style="color: var(--color-lightmode-primary);">About</span> <span class="brand-nano">Nano</span><span class="brand-know">Know</span><sup class="brand-tm">™</sup>' description='<span style="font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: 0;">An open AI and data-sharing platform for nanofabrication knowledge.</span>'>

<div class="prose dark:prose-invert max-w-4xl mx-auto mb-16 text-lg text-left">

NanoKnow helps labs, researchers, students, and facility staff organize, discover, and reuse the knowledge and data needed to train users, operate tools, develop processes, troubleshoot problems, and support complex research.

Today, much of this knowledge is scattered across documents, websites, internal folders, staff experience, and informal conversations. NanoKnow brings it together in a structured, searchable, and AI-accessible form so that expertise can be preserved, shared, and reused across the broader nanofabrication community.

By connecting documentation, process data, training materials, troubleshooting insights, and community expertise, NanoKnow helps reduce duplicated effort, accelerate research, improve training, and support the path from academic discovery to real-world technology development.

</div>

</Section>

<Section title='<span style="color: var(--color-lightmode-primary);">Why</span> <span class="brand-nano">Nano</span><span class="brand-know">Know</span><sup class="brand-tm">™</sup> <span style="color: var(--color-lightmode-primary);">?</span>' description='<span style="font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: 0;">Turning scattered nanofabrication knowledge into shared progress.</span>'>

<div class="prose dark:prose-invert max-w-4xl mx-auto mb-16 mt-10 text-lg text-left">
  <p>
    Nanofabrication facilities generate enormous practical knowledge every day — how to train users, operate tools, develop processes, troubleshoot failures, and support complex research. But much of this knowledge remains local and difficult to find: scattered across internal documents, websites, folders, manuals, staff experience, and informal conversations.
  </p>
  <p>
    Across the national nanofabrication ecosystem, many facilities face similar challenges. When knowledge is hard to access, even within a single facility, staff and researchers spend valuable time rebuilding documentation, repeating process development, and solving problems that others have already encountered.
  </p>
  <p>
    NanoKnow helps change this by creating an open, AI-enabled platform for organizing and sharing fabrication knowledge across institutions. By making documentation, training resources, process expertise, and troubleshooting insight easier to discover and reuse, NanoKnow can help accelerate research, improve training, reduce duplicated effort, and support the transition from academic discovery to real-world technology development.
  </p>
</div>

</Section>
